Fried Rice

Description

This Chinese-inspired fried rice recipe is my absolute fave. It’s quick and easy to make, customizable with any of your favorite mix-ins, and so irresistibly delicious.

Ingredients

  • Cooked, chilled rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce

    • Steps

    1. Use cold rice: You’ve gotta plan ahead and use thoroughly-chilled cooked rice. A fresh batch of warm (or even lukewarm) rice will not fry well when it hits the hot pan, and will result in soggy and sticky clumps — no good. So leftover refrigerated rice is ideal!
    2. Use butter: Yes, butter. I have made many a batch of fried rice using various oils, and I’m now convinced there’s a reason why Japanese steak houses use that big slab of butter when they’re making fried rice.
    3. Use veggies: This is one of my big pet peeves with lame take-out fried rice — not enough veggies!
